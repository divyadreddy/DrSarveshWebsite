Healing is a Lifestyle: Rules for Long-Term Wellness

To maximize the benefits of the Vedic Therapy, patients must adhere to specific lifestyle and dietary principles.

Essential Rules for Lifelong Health

  1. Do not consume any fruits or heavy meals after 9:30 PM.
  2. Drink water 45 minutes before or 45 minutes after a meal. Never drink during a meal.
  3. Avoid tea and coffee. If you must have them, wait at least 10 minutes after a meal.
  4. Always consume salad (raw vegetables) before your main meal.
  5. Eat a light dinner by 7:00 PM.
  6. Incorporate a natural sweet (like jaggery/gud) into your breakfast and lunch.

What to Avoid

  • Bread, Biscuits, Chocolates
  • Ice Cream and Eggs
  • All products made from Fine Flour (Maida)
  • Puddings
  • Iodized Salt (White Salt)

Precautions During Vedic Therapy

  • Avoid: Lemon, all sour foods, Urad Dal (Black Gram), Brinjal (Eggplant), Peas, and Stale food.
  • Exercise: Do not perform any type of rotational exercises for the hands or feet.
